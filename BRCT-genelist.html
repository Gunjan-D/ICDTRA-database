List of Genes coding for BRCT domain

  • The BRCT (BRCA1 C Terminus) domain is a protein domain that is commonly found in a variety of proteins involved in DNA repair, cell cycle control, and signal transduction. It is named after its presence in the BRCA1 protein, which is associated with hereditary breast and ovarian cancer.
