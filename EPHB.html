List of EphBs Receptor interacting with Ephrin

  • Hepatocellular carcinoma receptors that produce erythropoietin comprise the largest known family of receptor tyrosine kinases in mammals. The Eph family of receptors, which were first identified in a human carcinoma cell line, is now known to comprise two classes of receptors made up of 9 EphA members and 5 EphB members, which are arranged according to sequence homology.
