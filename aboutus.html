About us:

Rationale for the database:

  1. No prior distinguished database
    There are numerous databases available in various Universities or Research Centers, none of these databases can be compared to others, such as the NCI.
  2. Nationwide accessibility
    The exisiting databases do not offer services to the community of cancer patients nationwide. .
  3. Visual learning
    None of the databases also contain information in the form of videos.
  4. Multi-lingual
    The exisitng databases provide information about cancer only English and not in regional languages from Asia and India.

Goals of the database:

This database aims to encompass the various domains required for Cancer studies. Such as:

Research: The vision to envolope Genomic, Proteomic and Structural information of cancer related genes and proteins for quick access to the researchers.
Education: The basic information to understand cancer and it's influence in the Indian subcontinent.
